How does it work?

Every story begins with a single opening sentence, generated by the platform.
Each participant can add up to one sentence to the story before passing it to the next user. To be able to contribute, participants must upload a photo of them with the previous player and an object chosen initially for exchange.

On another note, stories contain at least nine contributions in order to be closed and eventually revealed. From the third sentence onward, only a random selection of two to five words from the last one is shown. Using this limited information, each user writes a new sentence, shaping the narrative through imagination.
